PentaHex is a CTF team that participates in a variety of CTF challenges.

Cybersecurity is extremely important in a generation that's moving closer to computers being a part of everything we do. The idea behind CTF competitions is to encourage people to learn more about cybersecurity and white hat hacking.

With so many jobs opening up about cyber, it's increasingly important to gain cybersecurity skills.

We've created this site to help encourage others to participate in CTF challenges and learn about cybersecurity and related topics.
